Privacy Policy for Tycoon Game
Introduction
This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) outlines how personal data of users of the Tycoon game (“Game” or “App”) is processed. The data controller is Nekko Software, Górki Noteckie, Poland, referred to as the “Controller”. Data is collected solely to enable Game functionality, display rankings, and analyze activity for gameplay improvements and user experience.​

Collected Data and Purposes
The Game collects only essential data:

Device identifier or Google account – for authentication, ranking display, and progress sync.

Activity data (e.g., scores, sessions, interactions) – for analysis and gameplay optimization.

This data is not used for identity beyond the Game context and is not shared with third parties except Google Play services. Processing is based on user consent or contract necessity (Game functionality).​

Legal Basis and Data Sharing
Processing relies on Article 6(1)(b) GDPR (contract performance) and (f) (Controller's legitimate interest in analytics). Data may be shared with Google per their terms. No sensitive, financial, or location data is collected.​

User Rights and Storage
Users have rights to access, rectify, erase data, object, and withdraw consent (e.g., via in-Game account deletion). Data is stored during Game use plus 12 months for analytics, then deleted. Breach notifications occur within 72 hours.​

Security and Contact
Technical measures (encryption, anonymization) protect data. Policy effective from 02.12.2025. Contact: kontakt@nekko.software. Changes announced in-Game
